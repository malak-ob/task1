1. Pony Horses :

Ponies come in a variety of breeds and are known for their smaller stature and friendly nature. They are excellent for children and smaller riders.

2. Warmbloods Horses :

Warmbloods are known for their versatility and calm demeanor. They are commonly used in dressage, show jumping, and eventing.

3. Arabian Horses :

Arabians are one of the oldest horse breeds and are known for their endurance and distinct appearance. They excel in endurance riding and are also popular in various other disciplines.
